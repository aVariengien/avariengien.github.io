About

I work as a research intern at Redwood Research on language model interpretability. I'm a computer science master's student at ENS de Lyon in France, in a double diploma program at EPFL, in Switzerland. I'm a research enthusiast, developer, and graphical designer for fun. I'm always ready to wonder at the surprising complexity of reality.

My interests include AI safety and interpretability, biology, self-organized systems, and bio-inspired AI.
